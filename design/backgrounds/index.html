Repeatable Background Images

Tiled backgrounds go into and out of style fairly fast online. The primary rule: don't put text over a printed background. Text on solid colors, or subtle gradients, but not patterns.

These samples were fun to make because they helped me visualize how patterns form. It's like modern day quilting.
