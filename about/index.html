About

I'm a Software Developer that uses technology to solve interesting problems.

I developed my skills in a research role at BlackBerry as part of the Software and Design departments. There I helped pioneer a culture of intrapreneurship, helping take ideas from conception to reality – starting many successful products.

More recently I was engineer #1 at TrustPoint, a security and cryptography startup in Waterloo. I helped build a Certificate Authority as a Service, and an authenticity service for physical goods.

Web and Mobile are my primary focus. I have worked on mobile social networks, mapping, instant messaging, multi-device UI & communication, UI toolkits and made significant contributions to intellectual property portfolios.

I have a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Waterloo, and make use of it on a daily basis. I am fascinated with Macro Economics and what it teaches us about the world.
